About Palm Cove Tech Support

Who I Am

I am an independent technician with real repair experience focused on getting your devices working again quickly and affordably. I come directly to you, meaning there’s no need to travel or visit a shop. This makes the service especially convenient for those who prefer repairs at home or may have difficulty getting around. I specialise in repairing phones, tablets, and computers, handling everything from broken screens and battery issues to software faults, system cleanups, and device setup. The goal is simple: provide fast, honest repairs without the hassle or inflated prices you’ll often find elsewhere. Whether it’s a cracked screen, a slow laptop, or a device that just won’t turn on, I aim to diagnose the issue clearly and give you a straightforward solution. I also offer help with data transfers, email setup, virus removal, and general tech support—so you can get back to using your devices without stress. If you’ve got a problem with your tech, feel free to get in touch for a quick quote.

Since I don’t carry large amounts of stock, parts are ordered as needed to keep costs low and pass those savings on to you.

What I Help With

  • Screen & battery repairs
  • Charging port issues
  • Virus removal & system cleanup
  • Data transfer & back up
  • Device setup & email configuration
  • General tech troubleshooting
