Service Policy – Palm Cove Tech Support

Please read carefully before asking for a service.

💰 Pricing

  • Free quotes provided upfront
  • Repairs typically $60–$90+ for labour
  • Parts are charged separately
  • You will always approve costs before work begins

🔧 Parts & Payments

  • Parts must be paid upfront before ordering
  • All parts are ordered per job (no stock held)
  • Parts are non-refundable once ordered or installed

🛠️ Repairs & Diagnostics

  • Most issues can be quoted without a fee
  • Complex diagnostics may incur a fee if no repair is completed
  • No fix, no fee applies to labour only (parts not included)

⚠️ Repair Risks

  • Some repairs require screen removal
  • Screens may break during repair
  • Screen replacement costs are not covered

🔒 Warranty & Data

  • Warranty depends on parts and supplier
  • No warranty on water-damaged devices
  • Data loss is possible — please back up your device

🔐 Privacy & In-Home Service

  • Customer privacy is taken seriously — your personal information will never be shared, sold, or used for any other purpose
  • Any passwords or account access provided are used only for the requested service and are not stored or recorded
  • You are encouraged to change passwords after your service for added security
  • All work is carried out respectfully and professionally when visiting your home
  • No personal data, photos, or files will ever be accessed unless required for the service

What I Help With

  • Screen & battery repairs
  • Charging port issues
  • Virus removal & system cleanup
  • Data transfer & back up
  • Device setup & email configuration
  • General tech troubleshooting
